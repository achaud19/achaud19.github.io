How it Works

1. Research and Planning: The first step is to research and explore the different packages offered by AirSpace. Our website provides detailed information about the different trips we offer, including the cost, duration, and what's included.
2. Booking and Payment: Once you have decided on a package, you can book your trip and make the payment through our secure online booking system.
3. Training and Preparation: Before your trip, you will need to complete a series of training and preparation sessions. These sessions will include physical and mental conditioning, as well as training on the equipment and technology you will be using during your trip.
4. Pre-flight Medical Exam: Before the trip, you will need to pass a pre-flight medical examination to ensure that you are fit to travel to space.
5. Launch and Recovery: The day of your trip, you will be transported to the launch site where you will board the spacecraft and embark on your journey. Upon your return to Earth, you will go through the recovery process which includes a debriefing and re-adaptation to Earth's gravity.
6. Post-flight Support: After your trip, our team will be available to provide you with support and assistance as you adjust to being back on Earth. We will also provide you with a comprehensive trip report and memories of your journey in the form of videos, photos and other materials.
Throughout the entire process, our team will be available to answer any questions and provide support. We strive to make your space travel experience as safe, seamless, and enjoyable as possible.
