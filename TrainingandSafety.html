Training Program:
Our training program is specifically designed to prepare customers for the unique challenges of space travel. The program covers a wide range of topics including spacecraft systems, emergency procedures, and zero gravity adaptation. Each customer will receive hands-on training with our state-of-the-art simulators, allowing them to practice and become familiar with the spacecraft before their actual flight. Additionally, customers will be trained in the use of space suits, which are essential for performing spacewalks and other activities outside of the spacecraft.

Safety Measures:
AirSpace has implemented a number of safety measures to ensure the safety of our customers. Our spacecraft and launch vehicles are built and maintained to the highest standards and undergo regular inspections to ensure they are in top condition. We have a comprehensive emergency response plan in place that has been developed by experienced professionals. Additionally, our spacecraft are equipped with the latest life support systems and medical facilities that are staffed by trained medical personnel.

Medical Requirements:
To ensure the safety and well-being of our customers, we have certain medical requirements in place. Before the trip, all customers will be required to undergo a comprehensive physical and mental examination, and provide a medical history. We will also require customers to complete a fitness test to ensure they are able to handle the physical demands of space travel. Customers with certain medical conditions may not be able to take the trip.

Risk Disclaimer:
Space travel is inherently risky and customers should be aware that there is always a risk of injury or death associated with space travel. Before their trip, customers will be required to sign a waiver acknowledging the risks involved in space travel. This is a standard practice in the space tourism industry, and it is important for customers to understand and accept the risks before embarking on their journey.

Contact Information:
We understand that customers may have questions or concerns before and during their trip. That's why we have customer service representatives and emergency response teams available 24/7. Customers can reach us by email at info@airspace.com. We are dedicated to providing our customers with the best possible experience, and we will do everything we can to address any concerns or issues that may arise.
